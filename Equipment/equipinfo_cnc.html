Equipment Reservation - Computer Numerical Control(CNC) Machine

A CNC machine, also known as Computer Numerical Control, is a powerful tool used for creating intricate and precise designs. It operates by translating digital instructions into physical objects through precise control of cutting tools. In a makerspace CNC setup, you can expect to find the following:
We offer the following features at our makerspace:

  • Material Versatility: Our CNC machines work with a variety of materials like wood, plastic, acrylic, aluminum, and foams, depending on the machine's type and power.

  • Milling and Routing: CNC machines remove material and create precise shapes with their rotating cutting tools.

  • Beyond Cutting: Advanced CNC machines may offer additional functionalities such as drilling, engraving, or even 3D carving.
