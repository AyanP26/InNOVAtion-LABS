Equipment Reservation - ARTS and CRAFTS

InNOVALabs provides all equipment required for following activities:
  • Painting
  • Oil Pastels*
  • Color pencils
  • Realistic Drawing
  • Sewing Machines and Hand Stitching*
  • Origami*

- Painting Supplies: Makerspaces offer a wide range of painting supplies, such as acrylics, watercolors, and oils, allowing artists to experiment with different techniques and surfaces to bring their artistic visions to life. - Oil Pastels: Artists can utilize oil pastels as a vibrant and versatile medium to create textured artworks, with makerspaces providing a variety of colors for blending and layering to achieve unique effects. - Color Pencils: With precise control and vivid color saturation, color pencils are ideal for detailed illustrations and vibrant artworks, and makerspaces offer a wide selection of brands and techniques for artists to explore. - Realistic Drawing: Makerspaces support artists in honing their skills in realistic drawing by providing high-quality paper, graphite pencils, charcoal, and other drawing tools to practice shading, perspective, and proportion for lifelike representations. - Sewing and Stitching: Makerspaces cater to sewing enthusiasts with equipment like sewing machines and hand stitching supplies, allowing makers to explore various techniques and fabrics for creating unique and functional pieces. - Diverse artistic mediums: Makerspaces offer a wide array of artistic mediums and techniques, including painting, oil pastels, color pencils, realistic drawing, sewing machines, hand stitching, and origami, allowing for versatile creativity. - Affordability: Makerspaces provide a cost-effective platform for artistic exploration, granting individuals access to various tools and materials without the need to purchase them individually, making artistic expression more affordable. - Inclusivity: Makerspaces promote inclusivity in the arts by offering access to equipment such as sewing machines, drawing supplies, and origami paper, making artistic expression accessible to individuals from diverse backgrounds. - Skill development: Engaging in arts and crafts within makerspaces fosters skill enrichment in various artistic disciplines, empowering individuals to develop their talents and express themselves creatively through activities like painting, drawing, sewing, and origami. - Collaborative environment: Makerspaces serve as collaborative hubs where artists and crafters can come together to share techniques, exchange ideas, and collaborate on projects, enhancing the creative process through collective knowledge and inspiration.
