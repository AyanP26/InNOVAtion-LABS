Equipment Reservation - METAL WORKS



InNOVAtion Labs metalwork area is a haven for anyone with a metalworking project. It provides access to specialized tools, typically too expensive for home use, and a collaborative environment for both beginners and experienced metalworkers. Our tools include: Cutting: For precision cutting you'll find tools like mills, lathes, and bandsaws. Joining: Welders (MIG and TIG) are common for fusing pieces together. Oxyacetylene torches provide an alternative for cutting and heating metal. Shaping: Grinders smooth and polish cuts, while drill presses make holes and benders create curves. Other tools: You'll likely find additional tools like shears, cold saws, and forges depending on the makerspace. Knowledge Sharing: Many makerspaces require safety certifications before using certain equipment. This often comes with training classes, providing a foundation for safe and effective metalworking. Experienced members are often around to lend a hand or offer advice on your project.expand_more Projects: The possibilities in a metalwork space are vast. You could be crafting anything from sculptures and furniture to car parts and robots.
